About

I am a robotics and autonomy researcher-engineer with experience spanning AMRs, quadrupeds, drones, prime movers, cranes, and industrial automation systems. My work combines AI, perception, localization, navigation, and mission runtime design.

My research direction focuses on robust and human-centered embodied AI: systems that are technically strong, operationally transparent, and safe to supervise in complex field environments.
