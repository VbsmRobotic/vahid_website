Human-Centered Autonomy

Mission-level transparency and clean handoff design are key to trust in safety-critical robotics deployment.

In industrial autonomy, technical performance is necessary but not sufficient. Systems also need clear operator visibility: what the robot believes, what it plans next, and why fallback behavior was selected.

The most practical path is explicit task contracts, observable runtime states, and graceful handoff from autonomous mode to teleoperation. This creates robust human supervision without slowing field execution.
