Scalable Mission Intelligence

Reusable task contracts and diagnostics are the bridge from a single robot demo to multi-platform deployment.

Mission orchestration should define explicit preconditions, completion rules, and failure paths. This avoids hidden coupling between perception, planning, and execution layers.

When every task emits clear telemetry and outcome labels, teams can rapidly identify bottlenecks and transfer workflows across different robot platforms with much lower integration cost.
